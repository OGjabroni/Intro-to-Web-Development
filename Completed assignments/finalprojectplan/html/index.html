Project Proposal

Theme

For my final project, I will be creating a personal website for my girlfriend, and her potential business partner, personal blog about Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC), and as a vehicle for self promotion with their consulting aspirations, and potentially acquiring new customers beyond their current clientele.

Prime Objective

The primary objective of the page is to create a self promotional knowledge sharing platform with the secondary objective to sway potential customers away from the competition.

Audience

The ideal audience would be individuals or companies looking for support with SFMC, they would be already knowledgeable about what Salesforce is, but are possibly looking for help/support in implementation, best practises, and use cases for the technology.

Information Architecture

The site will be set up like many similar blog sites for self promotion and information sharing including:

  • Home page
  • 2 seperate blog post (articles) pages including a 3rd lorem ipsum blog
  • An about us page
  • A Contact us page with forms

The home page will be a basic introduction, what the site is about, and some imagery. The blog posts will be simple, text emphasized posts with corresponding imagery and links. The about us page with include a simple resume of each person, and portraits of the individuals. I'm hoping to make a contact form where people interested in subscribing to a hypothetical newsletter, or to contact them for help on certain topics or just a business inquiry.
